✨ Vision & Methodology

The 9-Point Kongo Cosmogram

Rather than telling a linear story, The Crossroads uses the ancient Kongo Cosmogram as a navigational guide. This 9-point system (East, North, West, South, Center, Lower, Higher, Center, Zig-Zag) works as:

  • A dance step sequence — literally choreographed movement
  • A hand motion pattern — embodied spiritual practice
  • A narrative architecture — guides how the story unfolds across 7 timelines
  • A universal thinking tool — applicable to any community, culture, location, or life journey

The Cosmogram is Not Dogma

It doesn't claim to answer everything. It's one of infinite ways to think.
It forces travelers to define direction, understand their position, and make collective decisions.
It's been tested iteratively for 6+ years. It works.

Why This Matters

Traditional storytelling centers linear narrative. The Crossroads centers participation, discovery, and spiritual grounding. People don't passively consume the story—they move through it, like the Time Warp dance at Rocky Horror. Only those who walk the full journey catch all the revelations (especially Tyra's hidden connection to becoming Bertha in the far future).

📅 6-Year Development Timeline

This isn't a project that was conceived and built in isolation. It's been tested, refined, performed, and iterated with real communities over six years.

2018-2019
Initial concept development. Evan begins testing the Kongo Cosmogram as a thinking tool with local community groups and at the Sanctuary.
2020-2021
First creative workshops. Story framework emerges. Characters and timelines begin to take shape. Pandemic pivot: work becomes more introspective and digital-first.
2022-2023
Music production begins. Partnership with Western States Center and Culture Lab established. 23 tracks recorded across multiple studios and cities. 100+ artists engaged.
2024 (Spring)
"Smoke & Mirrors" album released. Public debut of the core narrative through music. Street performances and story circles begin. Community feedback informs refinements.
2024 (Summer-Fall)
Live performances. School assembly with 200+ children (K-5th grade). Kids learn and sing "Shake the Field" on the spot. Testing shows the story works across ages and contexts.
2024 (Fall-Winter)
Document proof of concept. Gather artist profiles, testimonies, and community feedback. Begin building multimedia presentation materials.
2025 (Winter)
Website launch. Interactive cosmogram portal built. Character sketches created. Pitch documents prepared for funders and institutional partners.
2025 (Spring)
Phase 2 & 3 Planning. Sol R LLC established. Expansion to additional Black Belt communities. Technology infrastructure begins (e-waste recycling arm, Sanctuary expansion).

⚔ THE CROSSROADS ⚔

A Portal Across Time & Space | Montgomery, Alabama

This is The Crossroads. A spiritual gathering place where all timelines converge. The map shows Alabama's counties with seven allies scattered across the state. At the center— where Interstate 65 and Interstate 85 intersect in Montgomery—glows Africa itself, transformed into a golden portal.

Below the sacred fence, a sword grows upward through the wood. Trees frame the blade. A boombox sits on the grass. Mojo jars anchor the space. And underneath it all, Bertha— the emerald dragon—coils in power.

"THE CROSSROADS PRESENTS"

At the intersection of Interstate 65 and Interstate 85, in the heart of Montgomery, Africa itself appears and glows. A metal fence. A sword growing upward. A tree surrounding it. Mojo jars grounding the space. A boombox broadcasting resistance.

This is where the story happens. This is where choices are made. This is where old worlds end and new ones are born.

The Timelines

⚡ EAST: 2026 | The Uprising Exposed

Year of Rupture

Tyra Washington stands at the crossroads. An army of friends occupies the exits. A dragon stirs beneath the city. The moment of truth arrives.

🌱 NORTH: 1996 | Sanctuary Grows

Year of Foundation

Ms. Ora tends the wounds. Peppaman holds the center. A place begins to bloom. No one yet knows this will be the anchor point for what's coming.

⛓️ WEST: 1901 | Bertha Bound

Year of Binding

The dragon is bound. A constitution is written to exclude. Power is stolen, hidden, and buried in the ground beneath Montgomery.

🛣️ SOUTH: 1952-1969 | Highway Cuts

Year of Displacement

Families scatter. Communities split. Interstates divide neighborhoods. But underground networks remain. Resistance continues.

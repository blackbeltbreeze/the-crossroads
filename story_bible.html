Complete authoritative source materials for The Crossroads storyworld

These documents contain the complete architecture of The Crossroads—character relationships, timeline, cosmology, dialogue, scene structure, and the full omniscient narrative spanning 1833-3176.

Primary Documents

The Omniscient Telling (Third-Person Narrative)

A complete, coherent third-person narrative of the entire Crossroads storyworld from 1833 to 3176. Written in journalistic/epic prose. This is the story as Elliot might tell it, freed from the "I" perspective. Useful for understanding the full scope, for screenwriting, for understanding what happens between the scenes.

Format: .md (Markdown) | Size: ~40 pages

How to use: For writers completing gaps. For visual designers understanding the overall mood and arc. For anyone who needs the "full story" told straight through.

Note: To access, download from your project folder or request directly from evan@jubileemgm.org

Understanding the Documents

The Story Bible is written in three voices:

  • Jamal (Blue notes): 2026 editor commentary and story choices
  • Elliot (Purple notes): 3176 context and future information
  • Red text: Gaps that still need filling (marked clearly so you know what's incomplete)

This structure lets you see the story, the editorial choices, the future context, and the gaps all at once.

Using These Documents Ethically

If you're a collaborator: Use these freely. They're the foundation of your work. You own your contributions; the Bible is shared infrastructure.

If you're an educator: Use freely for teaching. Cite as "The Crossroads Story Bible, compiled by Evan Milligan."

If you're publishing/distributing: Get permission first. Email evan@jubileemgm.org. We're generous with permissions for community work, critical about extraction.

If you're adapting: Credit the source. We build with open hands, but we don't accept unattributed use of our work.
